PRIVACY POLICY FOR JINETE DRIVER APP

1. INTRODUCTION
VR Bharat operates the Jinete Driver mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our driver partner platform.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Personal Information:
- Phone number (for authentication and communication)
- Name and profile details
- College/institution affiliation
- Vehicle information (if applicable)

Location Data:
We collect real-time location data to:
- Show your position to passengers seeking rides
- Enable navigation to pickup and drop-off points
- Track active trips for safety and transparency
- Calculate trip distances and fares

Trip Data:
- Pickup and destination addresses
- Trip duration and distance
- Fare and payment information
- Trip history and earnings

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

- To connect you with passengers seeking rides
- To process trip payments and calculate earnings
- To provide navigation and route optimization
- To send ride requests and notifications
- To maintain safety and resolve disputes
- To improve our platform and services
- To comply with legal obligations

4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Our app uses the following third-party services:
- Firebase (Google) - Authentication and database
- Google Maps - Location, navigation, and directions
- Firebase Cloud Messaging - Push notifications for ride requests

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your data.

5. DATA SHARING

We share relevant trip information with:
- Passengers (your name, vehicle details, location during active trips)
- Emergency services (if required for safety)
- Legal authorities (when required by law)

6. DATA RETENTION

- Account data: Retained while your account is active
- Trip history: Retained for 1 year for dispute resolution
- Earnings data: Retained as required for tax compliance

7. DATA SECURITY

We implement appropriate security measures including:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure authentication via Firebase
- Limited access to personal data within our organization

8. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data and trip history
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your account and data
- Opt-out of non-essential notifications

9. BACKGROUND LOCATION

This app collects location data even when the app is closed or not in use to:
- Update your availability status for ride requests
- Provide accurate ETAs to passengers
You can disable this in your device settings, but it may affect app functionality.

10. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our service is intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

11. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or email.

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your rights:
Email: contact@vrbharat.tech
